# ADR-000: Release 1 is the complete bounded public metadata corpus

Status: accepted  
Date: 2026-07-11

Release 1 covers every candidate discovered through the declared, frozen union
of official public enumerators for the main `www.gov.uk` publishing estate.
It is metadata-led: complete bodies and independently operated `*.gov.uk`
domains are excluded. Every candidate receives exactly one represented, alias,
redirect-only, tombstone-only or exceptioned disposition.

This implements the later scope instruction recorded in the requirements
register and supersedes the original brief’s sample-versus-complete choice. A
representative fixture remains a pipeline test only.
